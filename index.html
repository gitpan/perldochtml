Core Perl Documentation (v5.6.1)

Every Every perl distribution comes with the following documentation, typically viewable through the standard man program, the slower and less flexible perldoc program, or as HTML. However, many programmers seem to have trouble finding that documentation, so here it all is. You may also fetch it manually as one gigantic GNU-zipped tarfile (1.6MB) for viewing on your local system.
